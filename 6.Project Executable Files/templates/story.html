The Story Behind Housing Trends

This analysis reveals fascinating insights about how various factors influence housing prices:

Key Findings:

  • Renovated homes command significantly higher prices than non-renovated ones
  • The number of bathrooms has a stronger correlation with price than bedrooms
  • Homes built in the last 20 years show different price patterns than older homes
  • Seasonal trends strongly affect both sales volume and prices

Recommendations:

  • Focus renovation efforts on bathrooms and kitchens for maximum ROI
  • Time listings to coincide with peak buying seasons
  • Price older homes differently based on their renovation status
